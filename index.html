Canning Myth of the Month

Myth: “You can just flip the jar upside down to seal it.”

Truth: This old trick might have worked for Grandma’s jelly, but it’s not considered safe today. Flipping jars doesn’t guarantee a proper vacuum seal and can lead to spoilage or contamination. For safe, long-term storage, always use a tested water bath or pressure canning method. Let your jars cool upright and undisturbed.
