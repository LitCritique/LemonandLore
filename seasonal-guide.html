Canning is at its best when nature is, too

Knowing your local harvest seasons is the secret ingredient to successful, stress-free preserving. When fruits and vegetables are in season, they're not only at their peak flavor, they're more affordable, more abundant, and easier to work with. That means fewer trips to speacialty stores, better texture and taste in your jars, and a whole lot more joy in the process!
